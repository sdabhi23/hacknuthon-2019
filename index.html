As innovation and entrepreneurship are at the peak in India, we, at Nirma University, are organizing HackNUthon 2019, a platform for identifying new and disruptive technologies and innovations to solve different challenges.

It's a non-stop 36 hours product development competition, where problem statements will be posed to participants for innovative solutions.

FAQ

  1. What is the deadline for idea submission?
  2. 6th March, 2019 (Will be extended if required)
  3. When will the shortlisted teams be declared?
  4. 8th March, 2019 (tentatively)
  5. Can I come up with my own problem statement?
  6. Yes. We encourage you to come up with your own problem statements provided they fall under the given themes.
  7. Who can attend?
  8. Anyone with an idea, interested in learning new technologies or interested in developing a new idea in general.
  9. What is the required team size?
  10. Maximum team size of 4 and a minimum of 2.
  11. Should the team members be from the same institute?
  12. Not necessary.
  13. What if I am a lone warrior?
  14. We cannot accomodate lone warriors.
  15. What kind of assistance we will get?
  16. You will get 3 meals, basic amenities (water, snacks, internet connection) and support from awesome mentors.
  17. What should I bring?
  18. A valid government issued photo ID and/or your college-issued photo ID, phone, laptop, chargers, an empty water bottle & ample of team spirit. You may also bring earphones/headphones if you wish (well music does play an important role). Besides, if you need any hardware components, you can bring them along.
  19. What things will be provided?
  20. Lodging, food, mattress for resting and internet access for the duration of the hackathon will be provided.
  21. What will the participants get?
  22. Knowledge, Experience, Guidance. Because these things are priceless :) Also, goodies from open source communities.
  23. What will the winner get?
  24. Knowledge, Experience, Guidance. Because these things are priceless :) Also, prizes of up to â‚¹10,000 and goodies from open source communities.
  25. Is it safe for female participants?
  26. For sure. There will be other female organisers for the entire duration of 36 hours. Also, faculties will be present for the entire duration and the university has round the clock on-campus security services.
  27. What is the selection process?
  28. In the first phase, participants are shortlisted on the basis of their technical profile, the novelty and feasibility of the idea.
  29. What I shouldn't bring?
  30. Drugs, Alcohol or any sort of weapons that can injure or are in any way harmful or addictive. If you are unsure, please ask us.
  31. Do I have to be from IT/CE/EC to participate?
  32. All branches are permitted. However, a background of basic coding is recommended.
  33. Can I leave the campus at night?
  34. No, all participants will have to stay inside campus grounds.
  35. What are rules and location of the venue?
  36. The location will be inside Nirma University. No one will be allowed to leave the venue before completion of Hackathon, not even at night.
  37. Can I start working on the hack beforehand?
  38. No. The entire development of the project must begin and end during the time of the HackNUthon.
